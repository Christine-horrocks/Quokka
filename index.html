A Little Bit About The Quokka!

Interesting Features

The quokka is a small macropod, which lives on a few island off the coast of Western Australia. It is a marsupial and so bloody cute. It's ridiculously cute features include:

  1. General fluffyness
  2. A permanent and adorable smile
  3. Tiny little arms and hands
